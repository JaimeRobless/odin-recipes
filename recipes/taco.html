Ingredients

  • Beer Bater
  • Taco Suace

  • Tortilla

  • Oil
  • Lemons

Steps

  1. Make beer batter
  2. Make Taco Sauce
  3. Heat oil in deep-fryer to 375 degrees F
  4. Coat fish pieces with flour
  5. Dip coated fish into the beer batter
  6. Fry fish until desired and let rest in a paper towel on a pan
  7. Put the taco together
